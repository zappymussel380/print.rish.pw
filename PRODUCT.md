# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People requesting small-batch or one-off FDM prints in and around Guwahati. They arrive with a model file and need to understand printability, time, material use, and price without learning slicer software.

## Product Purpose

Turn a customer model into a transparent quotation backed by a real OrcaSlicer toolpath. Success means the customer can upload, see trustworthy processing state, adjust supported print settings, and submit an itemised quote without uncertainty.

## Brand Personality

Precise, direct, workshop-practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, opaque lead-generation forms, decorative 3D imagery that hides the actual model, and loading states that imply progress without reporting real system work.

## Design Principles

- Show the model and the real processing state.
- Explain price through concrete material and machine data.
- Keep the quote workflow compact and task-focused.
- Make failure recoverable without exposing internal system details.
- Prefer earned trust over promotional claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. All workflows must remain keyboard-operable, retain visible focus, meet text contrast requirements, avoid color-only status communication, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
